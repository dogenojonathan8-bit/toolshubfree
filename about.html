About SmartToolsHub

SmartToolsHub is a free, independent collection of online calculators built to answer the everyday money questions real people ask: How much of my paycheck do I actually keep? How much house can I afford? What will this renovation cost? How much flooring do I need to buy?

Big banks and tax companies often hide these answers behind sign-up forms, sales calls, or paywalls. We built SmartToolsHub to give straight answers for free, with no account required, so you can make a confident decision in seconds.

What we offer

  • PayCalc Pro — estimate your take-home pay after taxes in all 50 states.
  • HomeBuy Calc — see how much house you can afford based on local taxes.
  • HomeFixCost — get ZIP-based renovation and repair estimates.
  • FloorCalc Pro — calculate exact flooring square footage and cost.
  • MoneyMap Pro — map your monthly money in, money out, and what's left.
  • HomeStyle AI — get quick home and renovation guidance.

Our promise on accuracy

We keep our calculators updated with current information, including the 2026 federal tax changes from the One, Big, Beautiful Bill. That said, every result is an estimate for general information only and is not financial, tax, or legal advice. Tax situations vary, so always confirm important numbers with your employer's HR department or a licensed professional before making a decision.

How we keep it free

SmartToolsHub is supported by advertising and affiliate partnerships. We display ads, and some links on the site are affiliate links — including through the Amazon Associates Program and CJ Affiliate — which means we may earn a commission if you make a purchase or sign up, at no extra cost to you. This support lets us keep every tool 100% free to use.
